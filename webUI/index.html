VNN Discord Bot

Commands

Green = Everyone can use
Black = Admins/Mods only

  • .avatar - When used, this will get your account's avatar.
    .avatar @USER#0000 - Gets the avatar of said user.
  • .age - Shows the age of your account.
    .age @USER#0000 - Shows the age of the account of said user.
  • .cat - Post a image of a cute cat.
  • .coinflip - Flip a coin! Heads or tails?
  • .dice - Role some dice!
  • .dog - Post a image of a cute dog.
  • .fact - This will post a random fact in the chat. Most likely will be in German.
  • .fortune - It's like a digital fortune cookie! :)
  • .ping - Pong.
  • .tyler - Post a random image of Tyler McVicker.
  • .tylerdrawing - Post a random drawing of Tyler McVicker. (Thanks, Milk!)
  • .tylernudes - Denied.
  • .spiderman - Post a random Spider-Man quote from the Sam Raimi/Tobey Maguire trilogy. Pizza time!
  • .joker - Post a random Joker quote from The Dark Knight (2007). Introduce a little anarchy!
  • .kick - Kick the user. This will post a kick message in the Mod Logs Channel.
  • .ban - Ban the user. This will post a ban message in the Mod Logs Channel.
  • .mute - Mute an user. (needs to be manually unmuted)
  • .unmute - Unmute an user.
  • .warn - Warn the user. This will post a warn message in the Mod Logs Channel.
  • .reload - For developers only. When you modify a command, you can use the reload command to clear the cache, allowing for the updates to take effect without a bot restart.
